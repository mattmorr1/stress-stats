Stress Stats

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Stress Stats ("the App") is a personal biometric analysis tool that uses data from connected wearable devices to detect patterns associated with physiological burnout. This policy explains what data is accessed, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Access

When you connect a wearable device account, the App requests access to:

  • Heart rate variability (HRV / RMSSD)
  • Resting heart rate
  • Recovery scores
  • Basic profile information (name, email) for account identification

This data is sourced directly from the WHOOP or Garmin APIs using OAuth 2.0 authorization. The App only requests the minimum scopes required to perform its analysis.

How We Use Your Data

Your biometric data is used solely to:

  • Compute readiness and burnout risk scores
  • Generate time-series forecasts of physiological load
  • Display historical trends in the App dashboard

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the real-time analysis described above.

Data Storage

The App runs locally on your device. Biometric data fetched from connected accounts is held in memory during your session and is not persisted to any external database or server. No biometric data is transmitted to third parties.

OAuth access tokens are stored in your local application session only and are discarded when the server process stops.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal or biometric data with any third party. The only outbound connections are to the official WHOOP and Garmin APIs to retrieve your own data.

Data Retention

Because no data is persisted outside your local session, there is nothing to retain or delete. Disconnecting your device account (via the App's settings) immediately removes the OAuth token from memory.

Your Rights

You may at any time:

  • Disconnect your WHOOP or Garmin account from within the App
  • Revoke the App's OAuth access directly in your WHOOP or Garmin account settings
  • Request information about what data was accessed during a session

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the App evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
